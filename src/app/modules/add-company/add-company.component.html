Steps for Importing a New Company:

  1. Download or export the PDF version of the annual financial statements (e.g. 10-K) of the desired company

  2. Edit the PDF file to remove all pages except for the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statements

  3. The PDF should include, at the minimum, the Income Statement and Balance Sheet

  4. The Income Statement and Balance Sheet should be on separate pages and should
    include at least two years' worth of financial information

  5. Upload the edited file by either dragging it into the box to the right or by using the Browse option

  6. Populate the fields listed above as it relates to key company attributes (e.g., Company name, Industry, etc.)
